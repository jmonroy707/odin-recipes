Lasagna

I have for you a quick and simple recipe o make lasagna. With just a couple of ingredients and an hour of your time you will achieve a delicious Lasagna.

Ingredients

  • 10 oz lasagna noodles
  • 1 1/2 lbs lean ground beef
  • 3 cups cottage cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp garlic pouder
  • 2 tbsp dried parsley
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (6oz)can of tomato paste
  • 2(6.5oz)cans of tomaot sauce
